Everything that ever fell from the sky

We've sent 7,000 rockets up. This is what came down on its own: every meteorite in the scientific record — 45,716 rocks from space, each dot sized by its mass. The bright embers were seen falling; the blue were found later, sometimes millennia later. Tap or hover any dot.
